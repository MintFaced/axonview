ULEZ reshaping air quality and commute patterns
London's Ultra Low Emission Zone expansion to 240km² in August 2023 marked one of the most ambitious urban air quality interventions globally. Road transport NO₂ levels fell 22% in the first year across monitored sites. Simultaneously, the city faces a parallel housing crisis — Inner London rents have breached £5,200/month medians, pushing demand into Zone 3–4 commuter corridors.
